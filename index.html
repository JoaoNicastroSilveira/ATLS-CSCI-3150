About Me

I’m João — equal parts poker nerd and code goblin. I love the strategy in poker: the reads, the math, the patience, and the way every decision adds up. I bring that same mindset into programming, where I’m constantly thinking about flow, structure, and long-term patterns.

This semester pushed me to write intentional, semantic, accessible HTML instead of “whatever works.” I’ve learned how much design choices impact usability, especially for people using assistive technology. Features like skip links, proper headings, and clear landmarks help create experiences that respect everyone. I'm proud of what I’ve built and excited to continue leveling up my UI/UX skills: cleaner layouts, elegant spacing, smart defaults, and less friction everywhere.
