What Is Universal Design?

Universal design means creating websites that people of all abilities can perceive, understand, navigate, and use.

The Four Principles

Perceivable

Information must be visible, audible, or otherwise perceivable to all users.

Operable

Users must be able to navigate and interact with all elements — including keyboard users.

Understandable

Content and behavior must be predictable, clear, and consistent.

Robust

Websites should work across browsers, devices, and assistive technologies.
